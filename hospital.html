ABOUT

The Health Check project is a user-friendly platform that assists individuals in determining potential health issues based on their symptoms. The project takes in symptoms as inputs and outputs a possible disease diagnosis along with relevant medication information. Additionally, the platform provides the locations of nearby hospitals and links to medical stores for convenient access to medical services and supplies. With this project, users can receive quick and accurate health assessments, making it easier to take control of their health

PROBLEM

The challenge of accurately diagnosing a disease based on symptoms often leads to individuals seeking multiple medical opinions,which not only incurs additional time and cost but also delays the start of the treatment process.To address this issue,we aim to develop a solution that can accurately match symptoms to diseases and provide recommendations for the best hospitals and treatments nearby,streamlining the diagnostic and treatment process for patients

SOLUTION

Our solution aims to address the problem of inaccurate disease diagnosis based on symptoms by utilizing a comprehensive database that incorporates patient information such as age, temperature, and symptoms. By analyzing this data, we can accurately match symptoms to diseases and provide the necessary medication, obtained through a thorough search of multiple credible websites. Our goal is to streamline the diagnostic process and ensure that patients receive the correct treatment quickly and efficiently.
